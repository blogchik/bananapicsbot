# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bananapics ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our Telegram bot service for AI image generation (the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Telegram Account Data:** When you use our bot, we collect your Telegram user ID, username, first name, and last name as provided by Telegram.
- **Language Preference:** Your selected language for interface localization (Uzbek, Russian, or English).
- **Content You Submit:** Text prompts and reference images you provide for image generation.
- **Payment Information:** Transaction details when you purchase credits via Telegram Stars (handled by Telegram's payment system).

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the Service, including generation requests, model selections, parameters chosen, and timestamps.
- **Transaction History:** Records of credit purchases, generations, referrals, and other account activities.
- **Technical Data:** IP addresses (temporary, for rate limiting and security), request IDs, error logs, and service performance metrics.

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Wavespeed API:** We use Wavespeed as our AI image generation provider. When you submit a generation request, your prompts and reference images are transmitted to Wavespeed for processing.
- **Telegram Platform:** Account information and payment processing are handled through Telegram's infrastructure.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Service Provision
- Process your image generation requests
- Manage your account and credit balance
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- Send service notifications and generation status updates

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
- Monitor and maintain service performance and reliability
- Develop new features and image processing tools
- Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms

### 2.3 Administrative Communications
- Send important updates about the Service
- Notify you about changes to our policies
- Provide broadcast messages (if you've consented to receive them)

### 2.4 Referral Program
- Track referral relationships and award bonuses
- Notify referrers when new users join through their link

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

### 3.1 Service Providers
- **Wavespeed API:** Your prompts and reference images are sent to Wavespeed for AI image generation.
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** We use hosting providers to store and process data (PostgreSQL, Redis).
- **Monitoring Services:** Error tracking and performance monitoring (Sentry, if configured).

### 3.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe such action is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Investigate fraud or security issues
- Enforce our Terms of Service

### 3.3 Business Transfers
If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Active Accounts
We retain your account data, generation history, and transaction records for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

### 4.2 Generated Content
- **Prompts and Parameters:** Stored indefinitely for service improvement and dispute resolution.
- **Generated Images:** Temporary URLs from Wavespeed expire according to their retention policy (typically 24-72 hours).
- **Reference Images:** File IDs and URLs are retained with generation records.

### 4.3 Deleted Accounts
If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or fraud prevention.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:** Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols.
- **Access Controls:** Database and API access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
- **Infrastructure Security:** Docker containerization, network isolation, and regular security updates.
- **Rate Limiting:** Protection against abuse and unauthorized access attempts.
- **Logging:** Security event monitoring and audit trails.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Correction
You can view your profile information, balance, and transaction history through the bot's interface. To update your information or correct inaccuracies, contact our support.

### 6.2 Data Deletion
You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Note that some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

### 6.3 Opt-Out of Communications
- **Broadcast Messages:** You can opt out of non-essential broadcast messages through bot settings or by contacting support.
- **Service Notifications:** Essential service notifications (e.g., generation status) cannot be disabled as they are necessary for Service operation.

### 6.4 GDPR Rights (for EU Users)
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Right to Restriction:** Limit how we use your data.
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

### 6.5 CCPA Rights (for California Users)
If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt-out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
